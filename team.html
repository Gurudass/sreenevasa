Dr. G. Srinivas Reddy

Specialist in paediatric Orthopaedic surgeon

Dr. G. Srinivas Reddy, with 23 years of experience, is a senior Orthopaedic and paediatric Orthopaedic surgeon at Srinivasa Neuro & Ortho Clinic in Bangalore. He specializes in joint replacement surgery and advanced trauma care, etc. Dr. Reddy and his team are dedicated to providing exceptional care, ensuring optimal outcomes and recovery for their patients.

Dr. Reddy, with Medical Registration No. 46531 from the Karnataka Medical Council since 1996, has been serving as a Consultant Orthopaedician since 2000. His expertise includes:

  • Joint Replacement Surgery
  • Advanced Trauma Care
  • Spine Surgery
  • Paediatric Orthopaedics
  • Arthritis Management
  • Sports Medicine
  • Treatment of Joint Problems

Qualifications:

  • MBBS, Adichunchanagiri Institute of Medical Sciences, Bellur
  • Diploma in Orthopaedics, KIMS, Bangalore
  • DNB in Orthopaedics, Hosmat Hospital, Bangalore

He is a member of several professional organizations, including:

  • Gosicon Member
  • AAOS: American Academy of Orthopaedic Surgeons
  • Bangalore Orthopaedic Society
  • Karnataka Orthopaedic Association
  • Indian Orthopaedic Association
