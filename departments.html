We Offer Low Cost Medical Services

Discover Our Services: From diagnostics to treatment, we offer a comprehensive range of compassionate care,
tailored to your needs, ensuring your health and well-being every step of the way.

Spinal Therapy

Spinal therapy involves treatments like physical therapy, chiropractic care, and spinal decompression to address back pain and spinal issues. It aims to restore function, alleviate pain, and improve overall spinal health.



Joint Replacement Surgery

Joint replacement surgery involves replacing a damaged or worn-out joint, such as the hip or knee, with an artificial implant. This procedure helps restore mobility, relieve pain, and improve the quality of life for patients with severe joint conditions.



Knee Osteotomy

Knee osteotomy is a surgical procedure that involves cutting and reshaping the bones around the knee to correct alignment issues and relieve pain. It is often used to treat osteoarthritis or other knee joint conditions, aiming to improve function and delay the need for a total knee replacement.



Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Sports injury rehabilitation focuses on recovering and restoring function after sports-related injuries through tailored exercises, manual therapy, and strength training. The goal is to promote healing, improve mobility, and help athletes return to their sport safely and effectively.




Advanced Therapies

Advanced therapies like ozone therapy, growth factor treatments, and stem cell therapy aim to enhance healing and repair in damaged tissues. These innovative approaches use biological substances to reduce inflammation, stimulate regeneration, and potentially offer alternatives to traditional treatments for various musculoskeletal conditions.



Paediatric Orthopaedics

Paediatric Orthopaedics focuses on diagnosing and treating musculoskeletal issues in children, including congenital conditions, growth-related problems, and sports injuries. The goal is to address these issues early to ensure proper development and function as the child grows.

Arthritis Management

Arthritis management involves a combination of treatments to alleviate symptoms, improve joint function, and enhance quality of life. This may include medication, physical therapy, lifestyle changes, and sometimes surgical interventions to manage pain and maintain joint mobility.



Emergency Trauma Management

Emergency trauma management focuses on the immediate assessment and treatment of severe injuries or trauma, often in a critical or life-threatening situation. It involves stabilizing the patient, addressing life-threatening conditions, and providing urgent care to prevent complications and promote recovery..
